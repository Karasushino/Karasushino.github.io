DX11 Shaders (C++ & HLSL)

Coded a dynamic graphical application utilizing DX11 shaders.

  • Trochoidal waves with correct lighting and receives shadows, watercolor based on depth. (with dynamic tessellation)
  • Tessellated terrain generation based on heightmaps.
  • Grass shader with wind simulation, random blade rotation, height and spacing per vertex.
  • Pointlights with shadow maps.
  • Directional light with depth maps.
  • Most parameters can be changed with ImGui in runtime.

UE4 Axe Throw (C++ & BP)

Implemented an Axe Throw mechanic heavily inspired on God of War.

  • Most of the implementation using C++.
  • Axe calculates the number of spins based on distance from player.
  • Axe corretly lodges on any surface, including angled surfaces.
  • Easily change curvature, speeds and rotations from the editor.
  • A lot of attention to detail and polish, the main purpose was to take a simple concept and make it complex.
  • My work is the axe behaviour and functionality in C++.
